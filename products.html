PPR Pipes

PPR pipes are suitable for hot and cold water supply systems in residential, commercial and project applications.

  • Common sizes: 20mm to 160mm
  • Pressure ratings: PN10, PN16, PN20, PN25
  • Colors: green, white, grey or customized
  • OEM / ODM packaging available

HDPE Pipes

HDPE pipes are widely used for water supply, drainage, irrigation and industrial pipeline projects.

  • Materials: PE80 / PE100 options
  • Common range: DN20 to DN630
  • Applications: water, irrigation, drainage
  • Flexible supply for project orders
