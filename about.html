About Lanzhi Pipe Technology

Your trusted partner for PPR and HDPE piping solutions since 2016.

Who We Are

Shandong Lanzhi Pipe Technology Co., Ltd. is a professional manufacturer specializing in PPR pipes, HDPE pipes and related fittings.

Founded in 2016, our factory is located in Linyi, Shandong, China. We operate a 2,000 square meter production site with around 20 skilled workers.

We provide durable and cost-effective piping solutions for water supply, plumbing, drainage, irrigation and industrial applications.
